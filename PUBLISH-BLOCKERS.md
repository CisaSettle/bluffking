## 1. Data provenance — `engine/data/preflop_v2.json` — ✅ RESOLVED (2026-07-01)

Resolved via clean-room regeneration: the ranges are generated by
`engine/examples/gen_preflop_ranges.rs` from BluffKing's own equity engine + a
CFR variant (byte-identically reproducible, no third-party charts/solver
outputs). `_source` reads "clean-room", `engine/tests/preflop_chart_contract.rs`
guards it, and `scripts/prepublish-check.sh` passes. Counsel-reviewed pre-release.

> _Former problem (history):_ `_source` once claimed derivation from third-party
> commercial poker-range products — a redistribution risk. Superseded by the
> clean-room generator above.
